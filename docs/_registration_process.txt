You (admin) create an Invitation
  Go to /admin/
  Open Invitations → Add
  Enter the user’s email, set invited_by (you), save
  You send the invite email
  In Invitations list, select the invite → Action: Send invite email

User receives a link like /accounts/invite/<token>/
  User registers from the invite link
  User clicks the link
The app stores the invite in their session and redirects them to allauth signup
Email is prefilled + locked to the invited email
User sets password (and any other required fields) and submits
Invite is marked as accepted
On successful signup, the invitation records accepted_user / accepted_at
User is immediately forced into Business Setup
After signup/login, your ActiveBusinessMiddleware detects no active business
User is redirected to /business/onboarding/
User creates their Business
They enter business name → submit
App creates Business + BusinessMembership (active)
User is logged out on completion
After business creation, the app logs them out
Redirects to login with a success message
User logs back in
Now they have an active business membership
They land in the app normally (no onboarding redirect)
